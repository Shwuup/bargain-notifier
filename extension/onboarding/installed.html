USAGE INSTRUCTIONS

STEP 1: Click the OzBargain Notifier icon

STEP 2: Type in the text field what keyword you want to look for and then press ENTER.

STEP 3: Repeat STEP 2 untill you're satisfied.

Bargain Notifier will then notify you if there are deals which include those keywords every half an hour.

FETCHING DEALS

STEP 1: Click the green OzBargain Notifier icon

STEP 2: Click Open all to open every deal for every keyword.

OR

Click a green keyword to open deals specific to that keyword.
